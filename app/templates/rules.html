Game Rules

Scoring

  • Each correct pick earns points depending on the round:
    • Round 1: 1 point + seed
    • Round 2: 2 points + seed
    • Round 3: 3 points + seed
    • Round 4: 4 points + seed
    • Round 5: 6 points + seed
    • Round 6: 10 points + seed
  • Maximum points are calculated based on remaining games and your picks.

Deadline

All picks must be submitted before the first game starts. After the deadline, no changes are allowed.
